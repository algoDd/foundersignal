# MarketPulse

### See how the market reacts to your product before you enter it.

Whether you're launching a startup, adding a feature, or entering a new market — MarketPulse tells you if the idea is worth building before you commit.

---

## The Problem

Most products fail not because of bad execution, but because nobody wanted them in the first place. Traditional market research is slow, expensive, and often biased. By the time you get real feedback, you've already built the wrong thing.

---

## What MarketPulse Does

Describe what you're building in plain language. MarketPulse runs a full validation pipeline and gives you back:

**Market Research**
Real-time data on market size, trends, and growth signals — so you know if there's a space worth entering.

**Competitor Analysis**
A clear map of who's already in the market, where they're strong, and where the gaps are.

**Simulated Customer Interviews**
AI-generated personas that reflect your target audience hold conversations about your idea. You get honest, character-consistent reactions — including the hard objections — before you've written a line of code.

**Visual Dashboards**
Signal distribution, competitive heat maps, decision scores, and more — all in one view so you can make a call quickly.

**Validation Score**
A composite score with a clear Go / Pivot / No-go verdict and the reasoning behind it.

---

## Who It's For

- **Founders** stress-testing an idea before raising or building
- **Product managers** evaluating a new feature against real user segments
- **Teams** who want a fast second opinion before committing a sprint

---

## How It Works

1. Describe your product or feature in a few sentences
2. MarketPulse runs a multi-agent pipeline in the background
3. You get a structured report with market data, competitor insights, and simulated customer feedback
4. Review the dashboards, read the interviews, and decide what to build
